Shipping Policy

1. Shipping Methods

We offer several shipping methods to accommodate your needs:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days
  • International Shipping: 7-14 business days depending on destination

2. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on the weight of your order and the shipping method selected. We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50.

3. Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. Processing time may be longer during peak seasons or promotional periods.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can track your package using the provided tracking number on our website or the carrier's website.

5. International Shipping

We ship to most countries worldwide. Please note that international shipments may be subject to customs fees, import duties, and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

6. Shipping Restrictions

Some items may have shipping restrictions due to size, weight, or content regulations. These restrictions will be noted on the product page.

7. Address Accuracy

Please ensure your shipping address is correct at the time of order placement. We are not responsible for orders shipped to incorrect addresses provided by the customer.

8. Undeliverable Packages

If a package is returned to us as undeliverable, we will contact you to arrange reshipment. Additional shipping fees may apply.

9. Shipping Delays

While we make every effort to deliver your order on time, factors beyond our control (such as weather, customs delays, or carrier issues) may affect delivery times.

10. Lost or Stolen Packages

ShopEasy is not responsible for packages that are lost or stolen after being delivered to the address provided. Please contact the shipping carrier to initiate a claim.

Returns & Refunds Policy

1. Return Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unused, in their original condition, and in the original packaging. Some items may be exempt from returns.

2. How to Initiate a Return

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and the items you wish to return. We will provide you with return instructions.

3. Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error or a defective product. We recommend using a trackable shipping service.

4. Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected, we will process your refund within 5 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

5. Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned, including perishable goods, personalized items, and intimate apparel. These items will be clearly marked on the product page.

6. Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. To exchange an item, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the desired item.

7. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We will arrange for a replacement or refund.

8. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund within 10 business days, please check with your bank or credit card company first. If the issue persists, contact us for assistance.
